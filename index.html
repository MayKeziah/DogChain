DogChain

in Spring 2021 at the University of Washington Bothell.

  • Play Now

How to play


Health bar

Do nothing and the bar will depleat by 1% every second. Press space and it will depleat 15% per second. Press 'C' to move to the next scene.

Player movement

Click anywhere on the screen to make the white box move. Press 'C' to move to the next scene.

Movement through friction zone

Click anywhere on the screen to make the white box move. Move through the red box to see a change in deceleration rate. Press 'C' to move to the next scene.

Friction zone generation

Press space to generate a new friction zone. Press 'C' to move to the next scene.

Unlocking walls

Press space to unlock a wall. Press 'C' to move to the next scene.

Dog rescue

Use WASD to navigate. Get close to a green box to make it follow you. Collide with the grey box to destroy all green boxes that are following you. Press 'C' to move to the next scene.
